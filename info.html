About Me

My name is David Lewis. I am an african american male who is 17 years old and a rising senior of Pike High School. Some of my hobbies include playing tennis and music. I am currently a part of my school's varsity tennis team and last year I (and my team) played with a record of 11-5 as a second singles player. I also play in my school's jazz band. I play trombone and we play all sorts of fun songs over the school year.

I know a little spanish and I am currently taking an ASL Course over the summer. I have taken both software development and website database development as classes. In those classes I have won the first place awards in the IDEW(Informatics Diversity-Enhanced Workforce) Competitions in both classes. For one I created a proc website to help doctors get into contact with their patients online and with other doctors to make information easier and less of a physical hassle witch was for my website and database development class. For my software development class I created a short 2D scroller video game where you defeat bosses to get the the town and face the final boss with lasers shields and parry mechanics.

Outside of school I participated in multiple programs. Some of witch include BDPA(Black Data Processing Associates). I go to their scheduled classes every friday where we are taught about different types of code. I have been going there for 5 years and am in the advanced class. We also have an annual meeting witch a select few are invited to witch this year and last year was hosted at Atlanta, Georgia. This is an all expenses paid trip where you compete with other sections in BDPA in a challenge of your choosing. This also gives you a chance to see different famous well-built black tech companies and meet famous people. It is also a way to see different companies and colleges giving over the summer programs that can help you further your career.

Another program that I have participated in is CLD(center of leadership development). This was a weekly program in which we got together and learned about how to be presentable and to hold the respected CLD values. On top of this CLD is connected with hundreds of companies and Colleges, and can provide scholarships and business opportunities for people who show their excellence in their ability to hold onto those principles.

Most of the jobs I have taken have either delt with a type of sport or tech. My first two jobs were caddie at a golf course and being a junior tennis instructor. The other job I had was with TeenWorks in which I learned about physical engineering in a a hybrid work class environment. And finally my last work experience would be in Nextech the program I am currently in.
